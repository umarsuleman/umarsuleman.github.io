About Me

I am a PhD in AI and Robot Learning (LUMS) with over two decades across software industry and academia. I have founded many companies, served as Director at Automatrix Pakistan, and consulted for multiple companies in embedded AI/ML, computer vision, IoT, and cybersecurity. I have mentored startups, led R&D, and taught at FAST‑NU, UCP, UMT, and ITU, focusing on real-world innovation and productization.

Education: PhD (LUMS, 2006–2013); MS & BS (FAST‑NUCES, 1997–2003).
